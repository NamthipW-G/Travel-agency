Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for the best availability and prices. However, we can accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What is included in your travel packages?

Our packages typically include accommodations, transportation, guided tours, and some meals. Specific inclusions vary by package and are clearly listed on each package page.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options to protect your investment. We highly recommend purchasing travel insurance for international trips.

Can I customize my travel package?

We specialize in creating personalized travel experiences. Contact us to discuss your preferences and we'll create a custom itinerary just for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by package and destination. Generally, we offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before departure with full refund. Please check specific terms for each package.
