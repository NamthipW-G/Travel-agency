About Journii

Crafting extraordinary travel experiences since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate travelers, Journii was born from a simple belief: that travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and create lasting memories.

What started as a small team of travel enthusiasts has grown into a global community of explorers, local guides, and adventure seekers. We've helped over 50,000 travelers discover the world's most incredible destinations, from hidden gems to iconic landmarks.

Our mission remains unchanged: to make extraordinary travel accessible to everyone, while supporting local communities and preserving the natural beauty of our planet.
